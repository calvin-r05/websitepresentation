More info on dragon trees

The Dragon Tree or the Dragon Blood Tree, is characterized by its umbrella-like canopy, supported by a thick, singular trunk that branches out with age. The bark is smooth and gray, resembling elephant skin. Its leaves are long, narrow, and sword-shaped, arranged in rosettes at the branch tips, with a deep green color that can turn bluish-green under full sun exposure. It's resin is of a deep red which is used medicinally and in many dyes which is why this tree is endangered.
